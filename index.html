My favourite animal - Cocker Spaniel dog

Basic information about this beautiful creature

Cocker spaniels are known for being gentle, easy-going and affectionate yet lively. They are generally considered good with children. They tend to be non-aggressive toward other animals and people, but that also means they are not particularly good watchdogs.The coat comes in enough colors and patterns to please any taste. The well-balanced body is sturdy and solid, and these quick, durable gundogs move with a smooth, easy gait.They are easily trained as companions and athletes. They are big enough to be sporty, but compact enough to be portable.

These are several breeds od spaniels

  • English Cocker Spaniel
  • American Cocker Spaniel
  • Irish Water Spaniel
  • King Charles Spaniel
  • Field Spaniel
